Spicy Orange Sauce

This sauce goes great with most meats and vegetables. Use it to coat some chicken wings or as a sauce for your favorite stirfry.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Orange Juice
  • 2 Cups Soy Sauce
  • 2 Cups Mae Ploy Sweet Chili Sauce
  • 1 Cup white Sugar
  • 1 Yellow Onion, Minced
  • 6-8 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Medium Ginger Root, Minced
  • 2 Tbsp Neutral Oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a medium sauce pan over medium heat, then add minced vegetables.
  2. Cook vegetables till they start to develop color then add the rest of the ingredients
  3. Bring to a boil then lower to a simmer for fifteen or until thickened
  4. (Optional) Add 1 tbsp of cornstarch to thicken more
